Tortilla Recipe

Description

In Spain, a tortilla is a kind of omelette, with ingredients added - frequently sliced cooked potato and onion. However, over in Mexico, a tortilla is a kind of flat unleavened bread.

Ingredients

  • 200g all purpose flour
  • 150ml warm water
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl taking care to ensure it's well mixed into a ball of dough. Cover and leave for 20 mins
  2. Cut the dough up into roughly 6 small balls
  3. Roll the dough balls out so that they are flat, roughly 2mm or so thick
  4. Heat up frying pan on mid heat
  5. Place the flat dough onto pan
  6. Cook until one side is browned OR until tortilla is ballooning
  7. Flip and repeat
  8. Finally, take the tortilla out to cool down
  9. Serve once cooled and enjoy!
